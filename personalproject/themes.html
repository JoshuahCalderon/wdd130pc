Themes

Ever wanted your birthday cake to look like a boombox? No matter what you’ve wanted your cake to look like we make it our priority that it not only looks how you want it but tastes great too.

Marriage, one of the greatest moments in life. Seal the deal with the cake and catering that reflects the moment best. Whatever the color, flavor, number of layers or theme will make sure it’s something you’ll always remember.

K-12, college, university, or beyond, graduations are a symbol of completion and limitless potential. Complete your graduation with the decorations and catering of your choice! The possibilities are just as limitless as the future ahead.

Holidays are well spent with family and friends. Enjoy treats of your choice while celebrating any holiday or religious event that’s meaningful to you.

Want to cater the way you want it? No matter the event or reason ask Ida’s Sweet Creations. Anniversaries, game nights, community events, or ‘just because,’ we’ll make it happen.
